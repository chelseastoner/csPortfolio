This page is to describe my experience with CS! Growing up, I liked playing computer games like Scratch Jr., and in school, math was always one of my best subjects. I started in AP Computer Science and loved it! I realized that CS is something that I want to pursue as a career. I am currently applying to colleges, and CS is my intended major.
